Join us for 30 Days of Refreshing

30 Days of Refreshing

"Repent and turn to God, so that your sins may be wiped out, that times of refreshing may come from the Lord." - Acts 3:19

5:00 PM - 7:30 PM
Event Details

Join us for a powerful evening of spiritual renewal and repentance. This special event marks the beginning of our 30-day journey of refreshing, where we will focus on turning back to God and experiencing His divine refreshment in our lives.

Date: Sunday, June 22nd, 2025

Time: 5:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Location: Revival Palace Community Church, Gisozi-Musezero, kg706 ST, Kigali

Theme: Repentance and Spiritual Refreshing

Scripture Focus: Acts 3:19

What to Expect: Worship, Prayer, Teaching, and Personal Ministry

What to Bring: Bible, notebook, and a heart ready for renewal

Event Highlights:
  • Powerful worship and praise
  • Biblical teaching on repentance
  • Prayer and intercession
  • Personal ministry and prayer
  • Fellowship and community building
